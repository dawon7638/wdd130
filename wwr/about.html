History

Founded over a decade ago, Rapids White Water Rafting began with a passion for adventure and a love for the river. Since then, we’ve grown into a trusted company offering thrilling yet safe rafting experiences for families, friends, and adventure seekers alike.

What started as a small local rafting outfit has become a premier destination for white-water enthusiasts. Our team combines expertise, safety, and a deep respect for nature to deliver unforgettable river adventures.
